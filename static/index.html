Search Tips
  • Use quotes to search for exact phrases (this is very useful if a phrase contains spaces)
    Example:
    "2024 Topps Chrome" "Elly De La Cruz" refractor
  • Exclude unwanted results with a minus sign
    For example, this will exclude results containing the word "graded":
    "Elly De La Cruz" -graded
  • You can remove more than one thing at the same time
    Example:
    "Elly De La Cruz" -graded -auto -lot
  • Avoid removing very short words (they can hide good results)
    Instead of this:
    -you -pick
    Do this:
    -"you pick"
  • Card numbers give the cleanest results
    Example:
    "Topps Chrome" "USC159"
  • You can search for spelling variations by listing all of the spelling variations
    Example:
    "Topps Chrome" xfractor x-fractor retrofractor
  • Start simple, then add filters if needed
    Start with:
    "Topps Chrome" "Elly De La Cruz"
    Then refine to:
    "Topps Chrome" "Elly De La Cruz" "USC159" -graded -auto

Frequently Asked Questions

What are eBay comps for baseball cards?

eBay comps (comparables) are sold listings that show what similar baseball cards actually sold for on eBay. They provide the most accurate real-time market values for sports cards, including PSA graded cards, BGS graded cards, and raw ungraded cards.

How do I determine my baseball card's value?

Enter your card details (year, set, player, parallel type, and grade if applicable) into our search tool. We'll show you recent sold prices, market statistics, and Fair Market Value calculations based on real eBay sales data.

What's the difference between PSA, BGS, and SGC grading?

PSA (Professional Sports Authenticator), BGS (Beckett Grading Services), and SGC (Sportscard Guaranty) are the three major card grading companies. Each uses a 10-point scale, but market values can vary between graders. Use our Grading Intelligence feature to compare prices across all three.

Does grading increase baseball card value?

Yes, grading typically increases card value, especially for high grades (PSA 9-10, BGS 9.5-10). Our market intelligence shows the premium between raw and graded cards. For modern cards in gem mint condition, grading often provides 2-5x value increase.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes! Kuya Comps is completely free for baseball card collectors and dealers. Search unlimited cards, analyze market trends, and compare graded card values at no cost.

How accurate are the Fair Market Values?

Our FMV calculations use volume-weighted analysis, giving more importance to competitive auctions with multiple bids. We filter outliers and provide ranges for quick sales and patient sales. The more recent sales data available, the more accurate the valuation.
