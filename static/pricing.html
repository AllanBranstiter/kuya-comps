Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

You'll be charged monthly or annually depending on your chosen plan. All plans automatically renew unless you cancel. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your account page.

What happens if I cancel?

You'll keep access to your paid features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will revert to the free tier. Your collection data is always safe and accessible.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference immediately. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

What happens to my data if I exceed the free tier limits?

Your existing cards and searches are never deleted. On the free tier, once you hit 50 cards, you won't be able to add more until you upgrade or remove some. Daily search limits reset at midnight UTC.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on your first subscription purchase. Contact us at support@kuyacomps.com if you're not satisfied.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. We use Stripe for payment processing. We never see or store your credit card information. Stripe is PCI DSS compliant and used by millions of businesses worldwide.
